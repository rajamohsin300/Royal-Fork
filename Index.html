Our Story

Founded in 1985, The Golden Fork began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of continental fine dining to the heart of the city.

Our chefs use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that are as visually stunning as they are delicious. We believe that dining is not just about eating; it is an experience to be cherished.
